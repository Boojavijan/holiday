About Holiday

Holiday is a luxurious hotel and restaurant, a perfect retreat for relaxation and indulgence. Nestled amidst serene landscapes, we offer guests a blend of comfort, elegance, and unparalleled hospitality. Whether you're staying in one of our opulent rooms or dining at our gourmet restaurant, every detail is designed to make your experience truly exceptional.
