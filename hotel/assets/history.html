Our Journey: 2018 to 2025

2018

Founded our first restaurant and hostel. A small family-owned business focused on traditional and authentic flavors.

2019

Expanded the menu to include vegetarian and vegan options to cater to a broader audience.

2020

Launched online delivery services to meet the growing demand during the pandemic.

2021

Opened a new branch in a nearby city, with a state-of-the-art kitchen and improved customer service.

2022

Introduced a farm-to-table approach to ensure fresh, organic ingredients for all dishes.

2023

Won 'Best Restaurant' award for excellence in customer service and quality.

2024

Opened an expanded rooftop dining area for events and private gatherings.

2025

Preparing for an international expansion, bringing our brand and quality to new markets.
